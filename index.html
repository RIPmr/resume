Zoltan Hu

胡中天

Zoltan Hu, Master of Computer Graphics, 2021 Outstanding Master's Graduate of Zhejiang Province. Formerly served as the Chairman of the Student Branch of the China Computer Federation. Currently a Senior Technical Artist at NetEase Games Montreal. He was awarded the 2021 NetEase Technology Award, 6 patents obtained, and is experienced in shader and tool development, workflow optimization, rendering performance optimization, and CG animation production. Honest and cheerful, diligent and pragmatic, full of sense of responsibility, love the collective, happy to share, and have strong adaptability and learning ability.

   Project Experience

  • Justice Mobile

    Technical Artist

    2021 - Present

    Developed industry-leading solutions for the mobile game "Justice", including screen space smoke, facial expression animation retargeting, procedural generation of ancient Chinese buildings, and hierarchical costume binding workflows. These innovations enabled real-time interactive smoke simulation on mobile devices, facilitated the reuse of facial expression animation assets for any character, and improved the production efficiency of buildings and costumes by over 50%. Optimized shader performance on mobile devices by reducing instructions, achieving performance improvement of 25% on average for commonly used shaders. Participated multiple times in the production of promotional videos, with single video achieving over 2 million views. The shaders and tools produced have been adopted and extended across more than 8 projects, receiving high satisfaction from artists.

  • Moon Engine

    Open Source Engine Developer

    2015 - 2016

    Independently developed an open source rendering engine, with RHI layer and support both OpenGL and Vulkan backend. C++ reflection supported. Features implemented：Screen Space Smoke, SSGI, SSR, PBR, Disney BSDF: Clear Coat、Iridescence、Anisotropic, Unified Particle Based Physical System, Cascade Shadow Map, Screen Space Seperable Subsruface Scattering, Shader Parser (Support Include) & Material System, TAA, MSAA, Stochastic OIT, Alembic Support, GPU Skinning, GPU Driven Pipeline, Real-time Caustic, Component System, Post-Processing, Offline Raytracing Renderer, Spherical Harmonics Translucent, Mesh Editing, etc.

  • Web3D ocean information visualization platform

    Lead Programmer

    2018 - 2021

    WebGL based cross platform 3D ocean information visualization platform, as the project leader, designed the system framework and participated in the development. Successfully realized cross platform visualization of massive observation data.

  • Multi-source meteorological data visualization platform

    Graphics Engineer

    2018 - 2019
